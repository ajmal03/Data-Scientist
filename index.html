About Us

At Euphoria State, our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality education in machine learning and data science for everyone, with a special focus on empowering Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander communities. Founded by Andy Parackal, this initiative is driven by the vision of enabling individuals to understand, adapt to, and apply cutting-edge technologies in their personal and professional lives.
